About Us

OBJECTIVE:

To motivate and facilitate students to crack competitive exams

GUIDING PRINCIPLES

1. Forum provides an open environment for members to explore and interact on various career opportunities and knowledge enhancing sources to face competitive examinations.
2. It encourages peer learning to strengthen individualistic potential of the members.
3. The forum upholds the spirit of the competition by creating an atmosphere based on values and ethical practices.
